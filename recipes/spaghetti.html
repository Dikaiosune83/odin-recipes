Spaghetti

Description:

A beautiful mound of Spaghetti with Marinara Sauce and five meatballs. It is sprinkled with shredded parmesean and parsley flakes.


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Parmesean Cheese (shredded)
  • Parsley Flakes

Steps

  1. Boil Spaghetti noodles for 10 minutes or until al dente. Drain and cover.
  2. In a large pot, add Marinara Sauce and Meatballs, then cook on Medium heat for 7 minutes.
  3. Add noodles to Marinara Sauce and stir until well combined.
  4. Serve.
