Hot Dogs

Description:

Tender franks boiled and placed in soft, warm buns. Topped with chili, diced onion, and shredded cheddar cheese.


Ingredients

  • Hot Dog Franks
  • Hot Dog Buns
  • Chili (canned)
  • Cheddar Cheese (shredded)
  • 1 Onion (diced)

Steps

  1. Boil Hot Dog Franks for 10 minutes. Turn off heat and leave franks in the water.
  2. Pour contents of Chili can into a small pot and heat on medium-low for 5 minutes.
  3. Take 1 frank from the water, dry, and then put in 1 bun.
  4. Spoon Chili on top of frank, then sprinkle onion and cheddar cheese (as much as desired).
  5. Serve.
