Hamburgers

Description:

Medium-well hamburger on a seasame seed bun. Topped with American Cheese, lettuce, and tomato.


Ingredients

  • 1/4 pound hamburger patty
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Place patty on griddle and heat for 2-3 minutes. Flip patty over and repeat.
  2. Add 1 slice of American cheese on top of patty. Leave on griddle to melt the cheese.
  3. On a plate, add bottom of hamburger bun, then lettuce, then patty w/ cheese, then lettuce, then top bun.
  4. Serve.
